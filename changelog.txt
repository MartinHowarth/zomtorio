Version: 2.0.0
Date: 2026-06-25
  Major Features:
    - Complete rewrite for Factorio 2.1 + Space Age. Biters become an overwhelming zombie horde:
      far more numerous, individually weak, and represented by cheap "horde unit" clusters so huge
      numbers don't cost UPS.
    - Buildings destroyed by the horde spawn zombies equal to their total raw resource cost.
    - Infection: a hit from the horde infects buildings, robots, belts and inserters with damage
      over time; it spreads through the factory along the flow of goods (inserters, loaders, drills,
      belts). Full repair cures it. The player is infected by bites (shields protect) and cured by
      any net heal.
    - Corpse reanimation via the Space Age spoilage system: killed zombies drop burnable corpses
      that hatch into new zombies wherever they sit unless burned or kiln-dried first.
    - Telegraphed, evolution-scaling swarm events that strike at night, on top of pollution-driven
      attacks.
  Features:
    - Melee technologies: Tier 1 unlocks swarm multi-kill, Tier 2 strengthens it and unlocks the
      Double Tap toggle (clean, corpse-free kills).
    - Zombies move faster at night (configurable).
    - Denser, more aggressive nests and base expansion; pollution recruits far more attackers.
    - Oil in a building's cost spawns tougher zombie variants on its death.
    - Extensive configuration: horde size, active-zombie cap, infection on/off and time-to-death,
      player infection, night speedup, corpse reanimation on/off and timer, pollution cost per
      zombie, nest spawn rate, base expansion rate, night assault escalation, and swarm-event
      on/off, intensity and frequency.
  Info:
    - Requires the Space Age expansion. v1 acts on Nauvis only; other planets are unaffected.
